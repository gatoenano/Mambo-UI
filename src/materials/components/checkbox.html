The `checkbox` component with the :disabled property, appears with disabled style. Also, for error status in red.

.ui-checkbox_global: applies the color grey related to the "Global" section at the app.
.ui-checkbox_error: highlights an error showing the label and checkbox in red.
With the :disabled property, the checkbox element appears with disabled style.
