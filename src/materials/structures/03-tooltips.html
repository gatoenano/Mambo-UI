--- notes: |

The `tooltip` structure has some classes for different status:

.ui-tooltip-bg-dark: changes the background color to grey

.ui-tooltip-arrow-top & .ui-tooltip-arrow-top_*: always points the arrow to top
.ui-tooltip-arrow-top: puts the arrow at center of the tooltip container
.ui-tooltip-arrow-top_left: puts the arrow at left of the tooltip container
.ui-tooltip-arrow-top_right: puts the arrow at right of the tooltip container

.ui-tooltip-arrow-bottom & .ui-tooltip-arrow-bottom_*: always points the arrow to bottom
.ui-tooltip-arrow-bottom: puts the arrow at center of the tooltip container
.ui-tooltip-arrow-bottom_left: puts the arrow at left of the tooltip container
.ui-tooltip-arrow-bottom_right: puts the arrow at right of the tooltip container
